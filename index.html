The Universe

An endless expanse of mystery, beauty, and discovery.

What Is the Universe?

The universe is everything that exists: space, time, matter, energy, planets, stars, galaxies, and the laws that connect them. It is unimaginably vast and continues to expand, offering scientists and dreamers countless questions to explore.

Stars, Planets, and Galaxies

Within the universe, billions of galaxies contain billions of stars. Around many of these stars orbit planets, moons, and other celestial bodies. Our own solar system is only a tiny part of the Milky Way galaxy.

Why the Universe Inspires Us

Looking into the night sky reminds us how small we are and how much there is still to learn. The universe inspires curiosity, imagination, and a deeper appreciation for the beauty of existence.
